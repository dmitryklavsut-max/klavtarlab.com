01 / Projects

TelegaOS

A conversational operating system that turns Telegram into an AI-powered interface between humans and machines. No apps to install. No interfaces to learn. Just chat.

🌎

Agent Society

Under the hood: a self-organizing society of AI agents with social structure. Agents are born, evolve, compete, and die. Natural selection ensures the system optimizes itself. Governed by a Triad — Legislator, Executor, Judge — elected by performance metrics.
